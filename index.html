LOGO DESIGN

My logo symbolizes my clients love for fashion and sewing, and also art and design, while being customized for her initials. The main circle of my logo is a button, to represent fashion. I made use of negative space with the button, which makes it look like a C, representing my clients first initial. Then there is a needle poking into the button, and the thread coming out, curving around the needle, simultaneously blending into a paint stroke from the paintbrush, which also represents the H of my client's last name. Made on Illustrator. Completed: 02/12/24.
